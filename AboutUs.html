Our role in Politeknik Brunei

Politeknik Brunei's Human resources carries out many services for the school. They include:

Services :

  1. Recruitment and Training:
    • We are responsible for advertising vacancies, interviewing/hiring potential candidates and training them as soon they get started. New employees are made sure to be fully prepared to work when they complete their training and will serve up to the standards Politeknik expects.
  2. Managing employees:
    • Planning/coordinating employee workforce to utilize them to the best of their talents.
    • Resolve any issues risen between employees and management
    • Carry out any disciplinary measures if needed
    • Supervising staff and specialists
  3. Monetary compensation
    • Managing school payroll
    • Maintaining a balanced and fair payment system.
  4. Employee retention

    5. Maintaining staff morale is paramount to ensuring they keep working in Politeknik. We are responsible for making sure they remain happy working here by listening to and trying to solve any issues or problems they may have.

  5. Employee Performance Improvement Plans

    6. The human resources department is often instrumental in setting up performance improvement plans commonly called PIPs. In general, these are written proposals designed to help struggling employees improve their work to raise it to a certain expectation level of the company. The PIP may include a description of the behavior or performance that needs attention, objectives to be met within a certain time period, a plan for accomplishing the improvement along with support resources and detailed consequences if the improvement does not occur.

  6. Policy, Planning and Process Improvement:

    7. Development, planning and implementation of HR policies, procedures and best practices for the workforce including HR facilitators ourselves focusing on HR service delivery, business systems.

  7. HR reporting and data management

    8. Making sure HR transactions are processed in a timely and accurate manner with focus on integrity of data.

  8. Benefits and leave administarion

    9. The task of assisting employees with addressing any questions or issues they may have regarding given benefits, time reporting and payroll information.
